# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: [Date]

## Introduction

Photospots ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Information
- **What we collect**: Your device's location (latitude and longitude) when you use the app
- **Why we collect it**: To show you nearby photogenic spots and landmarks on the map
- **How we use it**: 
  - Display your location on the map
  - Find nearby spots based on your location
  - Provide distance information to spots
- **Data retention**: Location data is processed in real-time and not stored on our servers
- **Sharing**: We do not share your location data with third parties

### Device Information
- **What we collect**: Device type, operating system version, app version
- **Why we collect it**: To ensure app compatibility and improve user experience
- **How we use it**: For technical support and app improvements

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide location-based services
- Improve app functionality
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Ensure app security

## Data Storage and Security

- Location data is processed locally on your device
- We do not store your personal location history
- We use industry-standard security measures to protect any data we collect
- Your data is transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption

## Third-Party Services

### Mapbox
- We use Mapbox for map rendering and location services
- Mapbox's privacy policy: https://www.mapbox.com/legal/privacy
- Mapbox may collect certain usage data as outlined in their privacy policy

### Backend Services
- We use our own backend services to provide spot and photo data
- This data does not include personal information or location data

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of location services (via device settings)
- Contact us with privacy concerns

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
